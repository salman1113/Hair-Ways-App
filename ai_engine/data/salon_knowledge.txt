# Hair Ways Salon - Information Guide

## About Us
Welcome to Hair Ways! We are a premium digital-first men's grooming salon. Our goal is to provide a seamless booking experience and top-tier haircuts, beard trims, and styling. We use advanced computer vision AI to analyze face shapes and recommend the perfect styles for our clients.

## Booking & Cancellation Policies

- Booking: All appointments must be booked in advance through our website or mobile app. Walk-ins are generally not accepted unless there is a vacant slot, but we highly encourage booking online to guarantee your spot.
- Cancellations: We require at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment.
- Late fees/No Shows: Cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment or no-shows will be charged 50% of the service price. If you are running more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may be canceled to respect the time of the clients after you.
